I have provided an apk file ready for install on an android device.

Once opening the app you can login or sign up by clicking the the sign up button. 
Enter in the details and click sign up. Once signed up you will be taken to your account view page. 
Hit back to to go to the home page. 
Here you can see the current events in the database. 
You can create an event by hitting the plus sign. 
Enter in the details and hit submit. Then you will be sent to the home page. 
To view your event in the list click on refresh and you will see the event on the list. 
To view more details or edit the event click view. If you are the host you will have and edit button where you may edit or delete your event.
